## Red Flags — Call Vet Immediately

- Not eating for more than 24 hours
- Vomiting more than 2x in a day
- Straining to urinate or no urination in 12+ hours
- Skin turgor slow to return (dehydration sign)
- Open-mouth breathing or panting
- Extreme lethargy or won't move from one spot
- Sudden weight loss
- Pale or bluish gums

## How to Do the Skin Turgor Test

Gently pinch the skin on the back of the neck and release.
- Snaps back immediately = good hydration
- Slow to return (1–2 seconds) = possible dehydration — call vet
- Stays tented = dehydration — call vet now
