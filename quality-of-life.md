# Quality of Life Score — [Pet Name]

Based on the **HHHHHMM Scale** (Hurt, Hunger, Hydration, Hygiene, Happiness, Mobility, More Good Days).

Rate each category **1–10** (10 = excellent, 1 = very poor).

- **Above 35 out of 70** = Acceptable quality of life — continue current care
- **Below 35 out of 70** = Discuss comfort care or end-of-life options with your vet

Score monthly. Bring this to every vet appointment.

---

## Scoring Guide

| Category | Score 8–10 | Score 4–7 | Score 1–3 |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Hurt** | No signs of pain; comfortable | Occasional discomfort; managed | Constant pain or distress |
| **Hunger** | Eating well on own | Eating some; needs encouragement | Refusing food |
| **Hydration** | Drinking normally; skin turgor good | Reduced drinking; mild dehydration | Severely dehydrated |
| **Hygiene** | Grooming self; clean coat | Some matting; occasional help needed | Cannot groom; sores present |
| **Happiness** | Seeks interaction; shows interest | Quiet but responds when approached | Withdrawn; no response |
| **Mobility** | Moves freely | Some stiffness or hesitation | Cannot walk or move without pain |
| **More Good Days** | Clearly more good than bad | About even | More bad days than good |
